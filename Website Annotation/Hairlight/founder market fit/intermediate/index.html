How to Use

Step 1: Cleanse hair and scalp; hair may be dry or damp. Then remove the sealing caps from the selected DUO Pods. Insert it gently into the device until you hear a click.
Step 2: Long press the power button to start your treatment. Single click the power button to toggle through gentle, balance and boost mode.
Step 3: Gently glide the DUO from front to back of your scalp in a slow combing motion. Repeat until all desired areas have been treated.
